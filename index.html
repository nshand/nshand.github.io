ECE 5 · UC Santa Barbara · Team of 3

Looper Pedal

Designed and built a pedal-style audio looper that records up to four independent tracks, mixes them in real time, and outputs one combined signal. Features quantized looping synced to BPM and bar length, per-track mute and volume control via potentiometers, and a 3D-printed enclosure. Built around a Teensy 4.1 microcontroller with an audio shield, 8 momentary foot switches, XLR and 1/4" audio I/O, and SD card storage for .wav files.

Teensy 4.1 Arduino Audio DSP C++ 3D Printing SPI SD Card I/O
